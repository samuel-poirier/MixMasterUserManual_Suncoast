# Top-Off Terminal

**[Home](../index.md) > [Terminals](index.md) > Top-Off Terminal**

## Overview

The Top-Off Terminal is used by the operators working at the Top-Off station.

<img src="../images/terminals/topoff1.png" width="900" alt="Top Off Screen" />

- When top off cases are needed on a pallet, the pallet details appear on the terminal:

<img src="../images/terminals/topoffterm1.png" width="1400" alt="Top Off Screen" />

- Once the cases have been placed on the pallet, the top off operator must confirm them using the checkbox(es) on the lefthand side of the screen:

<img src="../images/terminals/topoffterm3.png" width="1400" alt="Top Off Screen" />

- When all cases have been confirmed, the **Pallet Completed** button will no longer be greyed out.
- The user must press this button, which will make the confirmation pop-up appear:

<img src="../images/terminals/topoffterm2.png" width="600" alt="Top Off Screen" />

- Once confirmed, the completed pallet will move forward towards the wrapper (if possible).
