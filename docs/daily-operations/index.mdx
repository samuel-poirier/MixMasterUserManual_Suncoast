## Startup

Complete checklist for beginning operations each day, including system verification, truckload preparation, and production launch.

### Pre-Production Checklist

Before launching production, verify the following:

**System Readiness:**

- MixMaster is accessible in the browser and you are logged in with the appropriate access level
- All sub-systems (InnoPick levels) are ready to go
- No critical priority alerts are displayed in the top banner
- Verify that there are no lingering Truckloads or Replenishments from previous production days that were not properly completed.

**Truckload Readiness:**

- Truckload data has been received from the client system and is visible on the [Truckloads](../truckloads/index.md) page
- Truckloads are in **Available** status
- Verify the delivery dates and truckload details match the production schedule

**Inventory and Replenishment Readiness:**

- Confirm that Depalletizing stations are staffed and ready to process replenishments
- If Manual Induction is being used, confirm the station is staffed

<hr style={{ border: "none", height: "3px", backgroundColor: "#747474", margin: "2em 0" }} />

### Launching Production

Once the pre-production checklist is complete:

1. Navigate to the [Truckloads](../truckloads/index.md) page

2. Create or verify [Truckload Groupings](../truckloads/truckload-groupings.md) according to the production schedule and priorities

3. Select the first group and to open the Launching and Resetting interface on the right-hand side of the screen
    - Note: To access the Launch option, you must click on the Truckload or Group Heading:

<img src="../images/daily-ops/tl-launch.png" width="600" alt="Truckload Launch" />

<hr style={{ border: "none", height: "1px", backgroundColor: "#747474", margin: "2em 0" }} />

4. Click **Launch**

<img src="../images/daily-ops/tl-launch1.png" width="400" alt="Truckload Launch" />

<hr style={{ border: "none", height: "1px", backgroundColor: "#747474", margin: "2em 0" }} />

5. Verify the truckloads change status from **Available** to **In Queue**

6. Confirm that replenishment requests begin appearing on the [Replenishments](../replenishments.md) page

7. Monitor the first few minutes of production to verify cases are being assigned to sub-systems correctly

**Important:**

- The first group of truckloads will be completed before the second group. Plan groupings accordingly.
- MixMaster may rearrange pallet order within a group to optimize production across sub-systems.
- If a truckload was launched by mistake and no pallets have started production, use **Safely Reset** to revert it to **Available** status.

<hr style={{ border: "none", height: "3px", backgroundColor: "#747474", margin: "2em 0" }} />

### Startup After Extended Downtime or System Updates

If the system has been down for several days or there have been system updates performed, extra caution is recommended:

1. Verify all sub-systems are online and communicating (check [Sub-System Status](../home-page/sub-system-status.md))
2. Check for any truckloads left in **In Progress** or **In Queue** status from before the downtime
3. Coordinate with supervision about whether to resume, reset, or delete incomplete truckloads
4. Launch a small test group first to verify end-to-end flow before committing to full production
